## Brand overview

Nigel's Kitchen Table is a warm, editorial recipe destination for home cooks and bakers who want dependable recipes, seasonal inspiration and practical kitchen knowledge.

**Brand line:** Seasonal recipes, beautiful bakes and practical kitchen knowledge, shared from Nigel's kitchen table.

### Brand promise

Every recipe or article should help the reader make something genuinely delicious with confidence.

### Core audience

- Home bakers looking for achievable cakes, cookies, pastry, desserts and bread.
- Seasonal cooks looking for relevant ingredients, occasions and collections.
- Readers who value ingredient guides, techniques, troubleshooting and practical Kitchen Notes.

### Brand personality

Warm, calm, confident, generous and practical - elegant without feeling exclusive. Food and useful content lead the presentation, while Nigel's portrait remains secondary.

## Content pillars

### Recipes

Evergreen, searchable recipes covering cakes, biscuits and cookies, brownies and bars, bread, pastry, desserts and puddings, ice cream, breakfast and dependable home cooking.

### Collections

Seasonal and occasion-led groupings that support discovery, internal linking, newsletters and campaign themes.

### Kitchen Notes

Practical supporting knowledge, including ingredient guides, techniques, seasonal produce, equipment, preserving, fermentation and troubleshooting.

## Partnership opportunities

### Sponsored recipe

An original seasonal recipe with editorial copy, food photography, website publication and optional newsletter or social support.

### Pinterest package

Branded pins and seasonal discovery content linked to an evergreen recipe, campaign page or partner destination.

### Newsletter feature

A considered editorial mention, recipe feature or partner placement inside the Nigel's Kitchen Table newsletter.

### Affiliate partnership

Clearly disclosed recommendations for genuinely useful ingredients, equipment, books and kitchen products.

### Seasonal collaboration

A collection, downloadable guide, ebook concept or campaign built around a relevant seasonal theme.

### Events and workshops

Future online or in-person cooking, baking or kitchen-table experiences where the partnership feels natural.

## Visual direction

Use the approved assets in [`docs/brand-assets/`](brand-assets/) and the rules in [`docs/brand-master.md`](brand-master.md).

- Warm natural light and realistic food texture.
- Olive and ivory as dominant colours, with restrained copper accents.
- Calm editorial spacing rather than crowded layouts.
- Food-led photography featuring desserts, bakes, bread, seasonal ingredients and practical kitchen details.
- Use the horizontal logo for presentations and emails.
- Use the NKT monogram for profile images and small square applications.
- Do not use wide promotional banners inside the portrait media-kit layout.
