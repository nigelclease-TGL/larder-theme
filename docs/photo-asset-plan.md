## Hero crop guidance

The mango cheesecake image is the strongest homepage hero. Because it is portrait, the desktop design should display it in a tall editorial panel rather than force a wide crop. Use `object-position: center 45%` and retain the cake, wooden board, flowers, passion fruit sauce and mustard textile.

## Alt-text drafts

- Mango and passion fruit cheesecake decorated with fresh mango, passion fruit, mint and whipped cream.
- Chocolate fudge layer cake with chocolate frosting and caramel shards.
- Carrot layer cake decorated with carrot ribbons and coconut.

- Cherry and almond bars stacked on a white plate.
- Chocolate crinkle cookies dusted with icing sugar.
- Rectangular homemade Biscoff biscuits cooling on a wire rack.
- Strawberry chiffon cake served with fresh strawberries and cream.
- Viennese biscuits dipped in chocolate and sprinkled with pistachios.
- Rustic artisan bread loaf sliced on a wooden board.

## WordPress upload process

1. Upload all ten originals to **Media → Add New** on the staging site.
2. Rename media titles to the recipe names above.
3. Add the drafted alt text and confirm each image is attached to the correct recipe post.
4. Set the mango cheesecake as the homepage hero in **Appearance → Customise → Nigel’s Kitchen Table Homepage**.
5. Regenerate thumbnails after activating the new theme so the `larder-card` and `larder-hero` sizes are created.

## Notes

The files are currently compressed to roughly web-sized dimensions. They are suitable for cards and portrait editorial features. Avoid enlarging them beyond their native width for full-bleed desktop use; use a split-layout hero instead.
